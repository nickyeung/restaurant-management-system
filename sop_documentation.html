🍽️ Food Handling & Storage SOP

Temperature Guidelines

All meat products must be stored at 32-39°F (0-4°C). Fish and seafood at 28-32°F. Frozen items at 0°F or below.

FIFO System (First In, First Out)

  1. Check expiration dates upon receiving
  2. Place older items in front, newer items in back
  3. Always use oldest stock first
  4. Discard any expired items immediately

Cross-Contamination Prevention

Raw meat must be stored below ready-to-eat foods. Use separate cutting boards for meat, vegetables, and other foods. Wash hands before handling different types of food.

🤝 Customer Service Standards SOP

Greeting Procedures

  1. Greet customers within 2 minutes of arrival
  2. Use friendly, professional tone
  3. Offer water and menu
  4. Take order after 5 minutes

Service Quality Standards

Food must be delivered within 15-20 minutes for main courses. Refill water glasses when 1/2 empty. Check in with customers after 2 minutes of receiving food.

Complaint Resolution

  1. Listen without interrupting
  2. Apologize sincerely
  3. Offer solution immediately (remake dish or discount)
  4. Follow up to ensure satisfaction

📋 Staff Training Checklist

New Hire Onboarding (Day 1)

  1. Complete HR paperwork
  2. Restaurant tour and orientation
  3. Safety and emergency procedures
  4. Uniform and equipment setup

On-the-Job Training (Week 1-2)

  1. Shadow experienced staff member
  2. Practice POS system
  3. Learn menu items and specials
  4. Customer service scenarios

Monthly Training Topics

Food safety, customer service excellence, upselling techniques, conflict resolution, and product knowledge updates.
